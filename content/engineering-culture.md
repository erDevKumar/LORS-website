---
title: Engineering Culture Principles
principles:
  - title: Absolute Code Ownership
    body: >-
      We build our own intellectual property. Our engineers don't clear external
      client tickets; they design architectural platforms they directly own.
  - title: Security-First Architecture
    body: >-
      Data integrity is paramount. All in-house applications feature military-grade
      AES-256 encryption routines and secure HTTPS configurations standard.
  - title: Optimization Bounds
    body: >-
      Our standard for frontend performance is demanding—rendering maps in under
      250 milliseconds and maximizing application performance while keeping active
      battery drain under 5% per hour.
---

Engineering culture at LORS Nexus is built on ownership, security, and performance.
